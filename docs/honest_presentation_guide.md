## 💡 **SAFE PRESENTATION STRATEGY**

### **Lead with the Concept**
> "SkyBridge Alaska is a community-powered mesh network designed to improve aviation safety in Alaska's challenging environment. Using proven LoRa technology and NASA's TAIGA Protocol, it can provide real-time information to pilots where traditional infrastructure is limited or expensive."

### **Focus on the Problem**
> "Alaska's unique geography and weather create aviation challenges that traditional infrastructure struggles to address. Remote areas need reliable communication, and pilots need real-time information for safety."

### **Present the Solution**
> "SkyBridge uses mesh networking technology that's already proven in other applications. It's designed to work in Alaska's conditions, requires no licensing, and can be deployed by communities."

### **Discuss the Benefits**
> "This approach offers several advantages: community ownership, no licensing requirements, solar power for remote deployment, and the ability to complement existing infrastructure rather than replace it."
