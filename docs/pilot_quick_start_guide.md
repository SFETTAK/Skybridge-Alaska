# SkyBridge Alaska - Pilot Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to the Alaska Aviation Mesh Network!

You're part of the initial testing group for SkyBridge Alaska - a resilient communication network designed by pilots, for pilots. This guide will get you up and running quickly.

## What's in Your Package

- [ ] Meshtastic radio device (T-Echo/T-Beam/Heltec)
- [ ] USB charging cable
- [ ] Quick reference card
- [ ] Optional: External antenna (if applicable)

## Initial Setup (5 minutes)

### 1. Charge Your Device
- Connect USB cable to any USB power source
- Full charge takes 2-4 hours
- Battery typically lasts 12-24 hours

### 2. Power On
- **T-Echo**: Press and hold side button for 3 seconds
- **T-Beam**: Press middle button briefly
- **Heltec**: Press PRG button briefly

### 3. Download the App
- **iPhone**: Search "Meshtastic" in App Store
- **Android**: Search "Meshtastic" on Google Play
- The app is FREE - no subscriptions

### 4. Connect Your Radio
1. Open Meshtastic app
2. Enable Bluetooth on your phone
3. Tap "+" to add device
4. Select your radio from the list
5. Enter PIN if prompted (default: 123456)

## You're Connected! Now What?

### Joining the Alaska Aviation Channel

Your radio is pre-configured with:
- **Channel**: AK-Aviation
- **Region**: US (915 MHz)
- **Role**: Basic pilot user

No additional setup needed!

### Basic Operations

#### Sending Messages
1. Tap "Messages" tab
2. Select "Primary Channel" 
3. Type your message
4. Tap send

**Message Ideas**:
- "N12345 departing Merrill Field"
- "Weather at Talkeetna - ceiling 2500, vis 10+"
- "Runway 7 at Willow icy, use caution"

#### Viewing the Map
1. Tap "Map" tab
2. You'll see other radios in range
3. Tap a node to see details
4. Your location shown if GPS enabled

#### Checking Who's Online
1. Tap "Nodes" tab
2. Green = Recently heard
3. Gray = Out of range
4. Shows distance and last heard time

✅ Test from your home airport

## Understanding Range

- **Ground level**: 1-2 miles typical
- **In flight**: 10-50+ miles possible
- **Mountain tops**: 100+ miles achieved
- **Urban areas**: 0.5-2 miles

Factors affecting range:
- Altitude (higher = better)
- Terrain (line of sight)
- Weather (minimal impact)
- Antenna orientation

## Battery Management

**Maximize Battery Life**:
- Dim the screen
- Reduce GPS updates
- Turn off when not flying
- Carry USB battery pack

**Low Battery Warning**:
- Device shows battery %
- App displays battery status
- Plan charging at 20%

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Connect to Radio
1. Ensure Bluetooth is ON
2. Restart the app
3. Restart the radio
4. Check battery level

### Not Seeing Other Nodes
1. You might be alone in range
2. Check antenna connection
3. Try from higher elevation
4. Ensure on correct channel

### Messages Not Sending
1. Check signal strength icon
2. Move to better location
3. Verify channel settings
4. Restart device
