# SkyBridge Alaska - Audience Handout
**Community-Powered Aviation Safety Mesh Network**

---

## 🎯 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SkyBridge Alaska is a revolutionary mesh networking solution designed to address Alaska's aviation safety crisis. Using NASA's TAIGA Protocol and LoRa technology, SkyBridge provides real-time weather, traffic, and safety information to pilots at a fraction of the cost of traditional infrastructure.

---

## 🚨 THE CRISIS

### **Safety Statistics**
- Alaska pilots face **significantly higher fatality rates** than the national average
- Multiple **RCO sites experience frequent outages**, leaving pilots without critical information
- **Below average ADS-B equipage** despite federal investment
- **High number of active NOTAMs** in Alaska airspace

### **Infrastructure Failures**
- Traditional RCO sites cost $550K+ per site over 5 years
- Frequent unscheduled outages impact pilot safety daily
- Limited real-time data visibility in remote areas
- High equipment costs deter adoption of alternatives

---

## 💡 THE SOLUTION

### **SkyBridge Technology**
- **NASA TAIGA Protocol** for efficient data compression
- **LoRa Mesh Networking** for community-powered communication
- **FCC Part 15 Compliant** - no licensing required
- **Solar-Powered** for remote deployment
- **Extended Range Capabilities** for Alaska's vast distances

### **How It Works**
1. Aircraft communicate through mesh nodes
2. Data flows through the network with efficient compression
3. Pilots receive real-time weather, traffic, and safety information
4. System operates independently of traditional infrastructure
