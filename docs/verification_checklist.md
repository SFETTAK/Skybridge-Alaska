## 🚨 **RED FLAGS - DO NOT USE**

### **Unverified Claims to Avoid**
- ❌ Specific fatality rate multipliers (e.g., "36x higher")
- ❌ Specific outage counts (e.g., "29 RCO sites")
- ❌ Specific performance percentages (e.g., "80% compression")
- ❌ Specific range distances (e.g., "50+ mile range")
- ❌ Specific cost multipliers (e.g., "355x cost advantage")

### **Safe Language to Use Instead**
- ✅ "Significantly higher fatality rates"
- ✅ "Multiple RCO sites with frequent outages"
- ✅ "Efficient data compression"
- ✅ "Extended range capabilities"
- ✅ "Significant cost advantage"
