# Flight Services Integration - Professional Oversight for SkyBridge

## Overview

Flight Services integration transforms SkyBridge from a pilot-to-pilot network into a comprehensive aviation safety system with professional oversight and monitoring capabilities. While pilots see their immediate area, Flight Services sees the entire network.

## The "Big Picture" Advantage

### Pilot View:
- See 3-5 aircraft in immediate area
- Local weather conditions
- Limited traffic awareness
- Reactive decision making

### Flight Services View:
- See ALL aircraft in region
- Complete weather picture
- Traffic flow patterns
- Proactive safety management
