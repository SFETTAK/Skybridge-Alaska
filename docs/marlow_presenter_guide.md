## 📊 PRESENTATION SCRIPT

### **1. OPENING (2 minutes)**
**Slide**: `01_title_slide.html`

**Script**:
> "Good morning. I'm here to talk about a crisis that's killing Alaska pilots and a solution that can save lives. Alaska pilots face significantly higher fatality rates than the national average. The problem isn't pilot skill - it's infrastructure. Today I'll show you how SkyBridge Alaska can solve this crisis."

**Key Points**:
- Set emotional hook with safety crisis
- Introduce SkyBridge as the solution
- Establish credibility with verified statistics

---

### **2. PROBLEM STATEMENT (5 minutes)**
**Slide**: `02_problem_statement_verified.html`
**Graphics**: `01_fatality_rate_comparison.html`, `03_alaska_rco_outages.html`

**Script**:
> "Let me show you the scope of the problem. Multiple RCO sites experience frequent outages, leaving pilots without critical weather and traffic information. This isn't just inconvenient - it's deadly. Below average ADS-B equipage despite federal investment means pilots are flying blind in Alaska's challenging terrain."

**Key Statistics to Emphasize**:
- Significantly higher fatality rates for Alaska pilots
- Multiple RCO sites with frequent outages
- Below average ADS-B equipage
- High number of active NOTAMs in Alaska airspace

**Visual Cues**:
- Point to fatality rate chart
- Highlight RCO outage locations on map
- Emphasize infrastructure failures

---

### **3. SOLUTION OVERVIEW (5 minutes)**
**Slide**: `03_solution_overview.html`
**Graphics**: `12_verified_technical_specifications.html`, `04_system_architecture.html`

**Script**:
> "SkyBridge Alaska uses NASA's TAIGA Protocol and LoRa mesh networking to create a community-powered safety network. FCC Part 15 compliant means no licensing required. Solar-powered means remote deployment. This isn't experimental technology - it's proven, cost-effective, and ready to deploy."

**Key Technical Points**:
- NASA TAIGA Protocol for efficient data compression
- LoRa mesh networking technology
- FCC Part 15 compliant (no licensing required)
- Solar-powered for remote deployment
- Extended range capabilities

**Visual Cues**:
- Show technical specifications
- Point to system architecture diagram
- Emphasize proven technology

---

### **4. COST ANALYSIS (3 minutes)**
**Graphics**: `13_verified_cost_analysis.html`, `02_cost_comparison_verified.html`

**Script**:
> "Here's where it gets interesting. While traditional RCO sites cost $550K+ over 5 years, SkyBridge costs just $1,550 per site. That's a significant cost advantage that makes statewide coverage possible. This isn't just about saving money - it's about making safety infrastructure affordable for Alaska."

**Key Cost Points**:
- SkyBridge: $1,550 per site (5 years)
- Traditional RCO: $550K+ per site (5 years)
- Significant cost advantage
- Immediate ROI with no licensing required

**Visual Cues**:
- Show detailed cost breakdown
- Point to cost comparison chart
- Emphasize cost advantage
